If you have been diagnosed or suspect that you have MdDS or Disembarkment Syn­drome, sign up for free member­ship in our private support groups. Two online support groups have been established for people who are interested in discussing MdDS. You may search and review thousands of messages from those who suffer from MdDS. There, you will find caring people who understand what it is like to live with this rare disorder; members are from all over the world. Please join us to share and learn from others.
Yahoo! Support Group
There are two ways to join this private group:
  • Send an email addressed to: mdds_support-subscribe@yahoogroups.com
  • Go to the support group’s home page here and click on the link, "Join This Group!"
      • Please note, there is an underscore ‘_’ between mdds and support in the above addresses. It is a required part of the email and URL addresses.
MdDS Friends on Facebook
Maintained separately from the public page (facebook.com/mddsfoundation.org), the MdDS Friends page is a Closed Group and access is by invitation only.

To request an invitation to the Closed Group, you must have a Facebook profile.

1. Log into your Facebook account.
2. Send a Friend Request
These two groups are managed by the MdDS Foundation and are for the sole purpose of discussing Mal de Debarquement Syndrome and related issues. Bulk emails and spam will not be tolerated and persons posting such items will be banned from the group. Members of the groups are not permitted to give out member addresses to third parties under any circumstances.
